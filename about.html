Packings & Jointings Gaskets Private Limited

Company Profile

Packings & Jointings Gaskets Private Limited started its manufacturing operations and is in 1999 is an API-6A Monogram Licensee and ISO 9001:2015 certified company. We have customers in India and also export our products to countries across the globe. Our products conform to international product standards and also to our Customer standard & specifications.

We have a state-of-the-art manufacturing facility with CNC Turning/ VMC Milling machines and Special Purpose Machines, experienced & trained personnel. Our investment in CAD/CAM software and CNC/VMC tool technologies, ensures that we can maintain the highstandards of quality and cost competitiveness and provide a low-cost solution for our customers sourcing strategy.

Strict quality assurance procedures are applied throughout production - from receipt of raw material, through manufacture to delivery with full material traceability. Our quality system adheres to the stringent requirements of API 6A / ISO 9001:20015. A full & comprehensive traceability, from material source with mill certification to final supply, is an essential ingredient in our company's strict quality assurance procedures.

Our Product Range includes:

  • Ring Gaskets / API Ring Gaskets / Ring Joint Gaskets
  • Metal Gaskets
  • Centrifugal Castings
  • Pump Wear Rings / Wear Rings
  • Heat Treatment
  • Valve Components
  • Overlay Welding
  • Precision Machined components / CNC Machined components
  • CNC Turning
  • VMC Milling
  • CNC Surface Grinding

Our Mission: " To provide value to the customer, grow a profitable business, train and reward people and adhere to health, safety and environmental responsibilities "
