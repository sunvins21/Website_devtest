CNC Machined Components /Precision Machined Components

Packings & Jointings Gaskets Private Limited (PJGPL) has been manufacturing CNC machined components /Precision machined components for over 20 years to major OEMs in oil and gas, valves and wellhead, pumps, wind energy, construction equipment and other engineering sectors. Our engineering experience, supported by our ISO 9001:2015 certification, provides a low-cost solution for our customers global sourcing strategy.

Our investment in CAD/CAM softwares and CNC/VMC machines / tool technology ensures that we can maintain high standards of quality and competitive costs.

Other in-house capabilities include surface grinding and overlay welding.

We have a comprehensive network of approved suppliers for special processes including laser cutting, water jet cutting, anodizing, powder coating, plating, passivation, nitriding, heat treatment and vacuum heat treatment.

CNC Turning CNC Milling Grades of Materials

CNC Turning

  • Conventional CNC turning centres, chuck or bar fed
  • Sliding head machines
  • Tolerances to 0.002" (0.05mm)
  • Max chuck diameter 14" (350mm)

CNC Milling

  • Vertical machining centres
  • 5 axis capability
  • Tolerances to 0.001"Max size 1000 x 1200mm

PJGPL works to customer drawing and specification requirements to develop the processes necessary to produce complete parts from a variety of material grades.

Grades of Materials: We manufacture valve components / valve trim parts in Alloy Steel, Carbon Steel, Stainless Steel(SS304, SS316, SS321, SS347, etc.), Alloy 20, Precipitation Hardening Stainless Steel (17-4pH), Duplex 2205(F51), Super Duplex 2507(F53), Inconel 625 and Inconel 825, Alloy 254(F44), Aluminium, Brass, Bronze, Aluminium Bronze, Nickel Aluminium Bronze, Nickel and Nickel alloys, Aluminium Bronze, Copper and Copper alloys, etc. We manufacture these from round bars, tubes, plates, sheets, forgings, ring rolling, centrifugal castings, continuous castings, etc.

We would be pleased to answer your questions on CNC machined components /precision machined components along with the manufacture and supply of CNC machined components /precision machined components as per your drawings and material specifications.
