Ring Gasket / Ring Joint Gasket are manufactured as per API 6A /ASME B16.20 specifications. We manufacture R series (Oval & Octagonal shape), RX Series & BX series in a variety of material grades (Table with Material Grade and Hardness provided on this page). The Gaskets are manufactured in-house on our CNC machines to adhere to stringent quality requirements and also to ensure to customers delivery commitment.

Low Carbon Steel & Soft Iron Ring Gasket are supplied in zinc electroplating coating (10 -12 Micron thickness).

We also manufacture Bonnet Gasket, Bonnet Sealing Ring, Transition Ring Gasket, Blind Ring Gasket, Rubber coated Ring Gasket. We can also supply Non-Standard Ring Gaskets as per customer drawing and material specifications.

We would be pleased to answer your questions on Ring Gaskets/ Ring Joint Gasket along with the manufacture & supply of Ring Gaskets/ Ring Joint Gasket
