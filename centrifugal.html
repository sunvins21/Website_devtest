Centrifugal Casting

Centrifugal Casting Heat Treatment Products



Centrifugal Casting is a process that produces castings of very good mechanical properties. In the centrifugal casting process, molten metal is poured into a spinning mould/die. The mould / die can be spinning either on a vertical or horizontal axis depending on the shape of the desired part. We have a state-of-the-art manufacturing facility with CNC Turning/ VMC Milling machines and Special Purpose Machines, experienced & trained personnel. Our investment in CAD/CAM software and CNC/VMC tool technologies, ensures that we can maintain the highstandards of quality and cost competitiveness and provide a low-cost solution for our customers sourcing strategy.
Due to the Centrifugal forces applied to the molten metal in the spinning die, impurities segregate towards the Inner Diameter which can be easily removed by machining. Also solidification takes place from the Outer Diameter to the Inner Diameter, this avoids any shrinkages, giving a defect-free casting without cavities or gas pockets.

Heat Treatment is supplied to the components, to meet customer specifications.Our plant is equipped with Heat Treatment furnaces with a capacity upto 1200 Kgs and a maximum temperature of 1200 C. All our temperature controllers, recorders, thermo-couples are calibrated once in every three months and the furnaces are calibrated once a year as per Annexure M (API Standards).We do Solution Annealing, Tempering, Hardening and other types of Heat Treatment.
